The New Cloud

We want to implement the first human-powered cloud.
The files you store online are only visible if you provide the power for it, with a simple dynamo attached to the screen. That way, the user doesn't rely on big Data Centers that use incredible amounts of energy, which is a growing problem considering the amount of devices we have and the many more to come.

We want to make people more aware of how data is handled and the overall impact it has on the World in terms of pollution and energy consumption.

What can you do now ?

Think about your data online, and what is necessary, what would really be worth your own energy. How many pictures would you delete from Instagram, Facebook or which ones will you be willing to power by hand ?
